Welcome to Cheese Run!
Your backstory:
You are a mouse running through the cave of magical cheese trying 
to collect the tastiest cheese in all of the lands.
Your goal:
collect as much cheese as possible to add to your score, but make 
sure not to run into any obstacles. You can jump onto an obstacle 
but if you run into it, unfortunately your time in the magical 
cave will end.
Tools:
Press the space bar to jump.
Press the 'esc' key to close the application.
Good luck!
